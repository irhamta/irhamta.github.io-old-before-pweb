    ABOUT ME

    I am an Observational Astronomer. My research interest is the multi-wavelength study of galaxies and active galactic nuclei, especially about their formation and evolution. I am also an experienced Data Scientist with a demonstrated history of working in the financial technology and e-commerce industry.

    I graduated with a Master's Degree in Astronomy from Institut Teknologi Bandung under supervision of Dr. rer. nat. M. Ikbal Arifyanto and Prof. Dr. Wolfram Kollatschny. My theses are titled "Study of Double-Peaked Emission Lines AGN as Binary AGN Candidate" and "On the Nature of Type 1 AGN: Emission Properties and Correlations".
