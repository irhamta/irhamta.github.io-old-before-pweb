ABOUT ME

Hello! I am an observational astronomer who work in understanding the formation and evolution of active galaxies at high redshift. My research is focused on the growth of supermassive black holes, their role in cosmic reionization, and the environments where they live in, when the universe was still very young. Currently, I am a PhD student of Max Planck Institute for Astronomy, working in the EUCLID group on finding and characterizing (gravitationally lensed) quasars at the epoch of reionization.

Outside astronomy, I am a Data Scientist with experience in delivering insights via data analytics and advanced machine learning for the fintech/e-commerce business, products, and market.
